About Us

Why Helping Hands?

We believe every unused item in your home can create happiness elsewhere. Our platform makes donating simple, transparent, and impactful.

Every year, millions of clothes, toys, and books go unused while countless families struggle to afford basic necessities. Helping Hands bridges this gap.

We provide a seamless platform to donate your gently used items. Just fill out a form, upload a photo, and our volunteer team will pick up the items from your doorstep.
